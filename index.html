ALICE is an intelligent conversational platform that drives growth and solve specific, high impact problems for Businesses.

The human-like environment


ALICE helps customers to blend into the conversation so that they can get their queries satisfied easily and effectively. The human-like environment guides users to place order or to get answers. Whatever your web or mobile application can do, ALICE can do too and much more!

ALICE segments your users


If you own a business, you can run specific campaigns to get new customers and retain old users. ALICE segments your users based on their interaction with her, analyse their pattern and push specific offers to segmented users directly to their messenger.

Follows-up


ALICE always follows-up with users who have interacted with her. Behind the hood, she analyses their history to get a better idea about them. This helps ALICE to push contents to users that has the maximum possibility to retain them back to the core service.
